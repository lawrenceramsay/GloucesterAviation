About

Who we are

We offer medicals for UK and US pilots, air traffic controllers and cabin crew. The medical team comprises Dr Ian Ramsay and Dr Emma Ramsay.Both of whom hold PPLs and enjoy flying Cessnas out of Gloucestershire Airport.

Medical Services

In addition to regular aviation medical services, Dr Ramsay offers support and consultation on all medical aspects that may have an impact on your flying licence.

Team

Dr Ian Ramsay

Founder

Dr Ian Ramsay is qualified to undertake EASA medicals, including revalidation of Class 1, initial and revalidation of Class 2, Class 3 and cabin crew medicals. He is also qualified to undertake revalidation of Class 1 FAA medicals. Dr Ramsay is an experienced AME, having performed aviation medicals for 30 years, and previous to this he was a GP principal in Cheltenham.

Dr Emma Ramsay

Partner

Dr Emma Ramsay is qualified to undertake initial and revalidation of Class 2 medicals. She has recently qualified as an AME and also works as a GP in Bristol.
